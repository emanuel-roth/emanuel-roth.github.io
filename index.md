<div id="describe-text">
	<p>My masters thesis was on canonical reductions of principal bundles, which is now a <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.17371v1">preprint</a> with Florent Schaffhauser. It explains how Harder-Narasimhan filtrations of vector bundles can be generalized to principal bundles, and works out some cool examples (symplectic and special orthogonal versions).</p>
